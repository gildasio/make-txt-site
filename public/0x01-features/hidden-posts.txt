---[[ 0x00 Intro ]]-------------------------------------------------------------

I know there are texts you don't want to be made public, me too. Because of
that, *Make TXT Site* provide this feature.

I will explain how to use hidden posts feature in *Make TXT Site*.

- 0x04 Signature

---[[ 0x02 Hidden Posts Feature ]]----------------------------------------------

First of all you must have configured some variables in `Makefile`, as discussed
in `/0x00-starting.txt`, they are:

- `HIDDEN_INDEX`
- `HIDDEN_PATTERN`

These variables are used to create the hidden posts lists and exclude hidden
posts from normal public index.

For example if you `HIDDEN_PATTERN` is `priv_` so create posts following that
pattern in posts directory and they will be treated as hidden posts:

- public/priv_example.txt
- public/priv_secret.txt

These posts will not be listed in normal public index but in hidden index as
well.

---[[ 0x03 Example ]]-----------------------------------------------------------

This is a hidden post:

- https://gildasio.gitlab.io/make-txt-site/priv_example.txt

And it isn't listed in public index:

- https://gildasio.gitlab.io/make-txt-site/index.txt

But in hidden index:

- https://gildasio.gitlab.io/make-txt-site/priv.txt
